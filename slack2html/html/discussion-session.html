mpmilano
2020-11-17 20:05
@jongold one interesting place to look for ideas of a resilient programming model is the PL-side work on NVRam. NVRam gives you an odd guarantee --- you can request persistence, but you also just get randomly persisted as the application runs. That means writing an application that can be resumed involves a whole lot of careful attention to what state is actually present, how it got there, and therefore where in the computation you should resume _from_. It seems pretty similar to the challenges you were talking about!
